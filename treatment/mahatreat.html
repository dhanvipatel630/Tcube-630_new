Deenanath Mangeshkar Hospital & Research Center is a prominent charitable multi-specialty hospital located in Erandwane, Pune, Maharashtra. Established in 2001, it is named after the esteemed Marathi theater actor and Hindustani classical vocalist, Deenanath Mangeshkar. The hospital is managed by the Lata Mangeshkar Medical Foundation and the Jnana Prabodhini Medical Trust.
