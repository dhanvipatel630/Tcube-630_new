Daal Bafla originated from Rajasthan and is a mouthwatering dish very similar to the Dal Bati of Rajasthan. Bafla is the cooking of wheat balls in pure ghee. These balls are served along with a bowl of Daal and coriander sauce to compliment the dish better. It is heavenly in taste providing a good dose of vitamins, minerals, proteins, and fats in the right quantities. The best Dal Baflas are found only in Madhya Pradesh.

Super healthy and light, Poha is a breakfast delight fondly served in many parts of the country. Poha is flattened rice with servings of onions, green chilies, and tomatoes with the best savoring of curry leaves and lemon. A bowl of Poha is quite fulfilling and enjoyed in all parts of Madhya Pradesh and India.

Palak Puri is a great combination of health and taste. Made with wheat flour, spinach is the main ingredient used in making these puris. The batter is deep-fried in olive oil once it is ready and the serving is done with aloo ki Sabzi or some amazing pickles. It is prepared during Navaratri for breakfast or packed in tiffin boxes. Palak Puri is good for health as Palak has the essential nutrients required for the growth of the body.

Malpua is a sweet dish of Madhya Pradesh made with wheat flour and sugar syrup. These flat wheat flour pieces of bread are deep-fried in oil and dipped in Chasni. It is best served by garnishing dry fruits on top of Malpua. These Malpuas are very yummy and extremely soft. Malpuas are essential items during Holi or other festivals. In some parts of Madhya Pradesh, Malpua is made using Moong Daal and sliced onions for a combination of sweet and salty.

Jalebis are sweet snacks for complimenting so many other dishes. The Jalebis are made of wheat flour and deep-fried in oil along with dipping in sugar syrup. It is dipped once the jalebis are crispy fried. There are many varieties of Jalebis like Cesar Jalebis, Mava Jalebis, etc. It is a famous dish eaten along with Poha or other such snacks of dishes. You can find a Jelabi vendor on every street in Madhya Pradesh.

Mawa Bati-With the right amount of sweetness, perfect crispness, and texture, MawaBati is one of the best sweet palates of Madhya Pradesh. The preparation includes Mava dough mixed with a generous amount of dry fruits. After frying the Mava balls, it is dipped in sugar syrup to add to the sweetness of the dish. This is one dish you cannot miss out on when in Madhya Pradesh.
